SQUIRREL PATROL

One minute. Three feeders. One garden hose.
💧 Hold & drag to aim the hose. The stream sweeps slowly — you can't be everywhere at once.
🐿️ Blast squirrels off the poles and across the yard. Soak them long enough to send them flying.
🐦 Mind the birds! Cardinals, chickadees, bluebirds & goldfinches earn points while they feed — but a faceful of water scares them off and costs you.
🛡️ Baffles slow squirrels down until they chew through them.
🐀 Gray squirrels are fast — they'll flank a feeder while you're busy elsewhere.
👹 Final 20 seconds — the Surge: giant squirrels need a long, steady soak.

Survive the minute with seed in any feeder and you win.
